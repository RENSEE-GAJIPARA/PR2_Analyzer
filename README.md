## 📌 Overview

The **Sales Analysis** workbook delivers a full-spectrum view of business performance across customers, regions, and product categories. With a built-in **What-If: Discount Impact on Profit** table and three dynamic charts, it gives sales teams and analysts everything they need to make confident, data-backed decisions — fast.

---

## ✨ Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| 📊 **KPI Banner** | Six headline metrics displayed at a glance |
| 🏆 **Top Customers Table** | Top 5 customers ranked by total sales with HV status |
| 🌍 **Regional Summary** | Sales & order counts across 5 regions |
| 🗂️ **Category Breakdown** | Revenue split across Books, Clothing, Electronics, Furniture & Office Supplies |
| 🔀 **What-If Analysis** | Profit projections at 0% – 30% discount levels |
| 📈 **Monthly Revenue Line Chart** | Revenue trend from Apr 2024 to Apr 2025 |
| 📊 **Sales by Product Category Bar Chart** | Category-level sales comparison |
| 🥧 **Sales by Region Pie Chart** | Visual regional revenue distribution |

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Download** the `PR2_Analyzer.xlsx` file
2. **Open** in Microsoft Excel (2016 or later recommended)
3. **Enable Content** if a security prompt appears
4. **Review KPIs** at the top for an instant business snapshot
5. **Explore** the Top Customers, Regional, and Category tables
6. **Use the What-If table** to model profit under different discount scenarios
7. **Analyse charts** — Bar, Line & Pie — for visual trends

---

## 📁 Data Dimensions

The underlying dataset includes the following fields:

- Customer Name & High-Value (HV) Status
- Product Category (Books / Clothing / Electronics / Furniture / Office Supplies)
- Region (Central / East / North / South / West)
- Order Date, Sales Amount, Profit & Discount Rate
